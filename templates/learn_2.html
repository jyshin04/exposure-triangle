What is the Exposure Triangle?

To take a well-exposed photo, your camera needs to control how much light reaches the sensor.

This is done using three settings:

  • Aperture
  • Shutter Speed
  • ISO

Together, these three form the Exposure Triangle. Changing one will affect the others. Understanding how they work helps you take sharper, brighter, or more creative photos.
