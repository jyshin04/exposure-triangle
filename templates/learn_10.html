Shutter Speed – Controlling Motion

Shutter speed is how long the shutter stays open to let in light.


  • Fast speed (e.g., 1/1000s) = freezes action
  • Slow speed (e.g., 1s) = creates motion blur

    • Tip!

    Use fast shutter speed for sports or action

    Use slow shutter speed for light trails or water blur

    Slower speeds can cause camera shake → use tripod
